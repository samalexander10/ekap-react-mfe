# Employee Benefits Overview

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Policy Owner:** Total Rewards / HR
**Review Cycle:** Annual

---

## 1. Introduction

We offer a comprehensive benefits package designed to support the health, financial security, and overall well-being of our employees and their families. Benefits eligibility is generally available to full-time employees working 30+ hours per week. Part-time employees may have access to a subset of benefits.

## 2. Health Insurance

### 2.1 Medical Coverage
- **Plans Available:** HMO, PPO, and High-Deductible Health Plan (HDHP) with HSA
- **Enrollment:** Within 30 days of hire or during annual Open Enrollment (November 1–30)
- **Employee Contribution:** Varies by plan and coverage tier (employee only, employee + spouse, family)
- **Effective Date:** First of the month following hire date

### 2.2 Dental Coverage
- Covers preventive care (100%), basic restorative (80%), and major restorative (50%)
- Annual maximum benefit: $2,000 per covered individual
- Orthodontia rider available with $1,500 lifetime maximum

### 2.3 Vision Coverage
- Annual eye exam: Covered in full (in-network)
- Frames or contacts: $150 allowance per year
- LASIK discount program through VSP network

### 2.4 Health Savings Account (HSA)
- Available with HDHP enrollment
- Company contributes $500/year (individual) or $1,000/year (family)
- 2024 IRS contribution limits: $4,150 (individual) / $8,300 (family)

## 3. Life and Disability Insurance

### 3.1 Life Insurance
- **Basic Life:** 2x annual salary, fully company-paid
- **Supplemental Life:** Up to 5x salary (employee-paid, subject to evidence of insurability)
- **Dependent Life:** Available for spouse and children (employee-paid)

### 3.2 Disability Insurance
- **Short-Term Disability (STD):** 60% of base salary up to 12 weeks
- **Long-Term Disability (LTD):** 60% of base salary after STD; continues until age 65 or recovery

## 4. Retirement Plans

### 4.1 401(k) Plan
- Eligible to enroll on first day of employment
- Employee contributions: Up to IRS limit ($23,000 for 2024; $30,500 if age 50+)
- **Company Match:** 100% of first 3% + 50% of next 2% of eligible compensation
- Vesting: Immediate for employee contributions; 3-year graded vesting for company match
- Investment options: 20+ fund choices including target-date funds

### 4.2 Financial Wellness
- Free access to financial planning consultations (2 sessions/year)
- Student loan repayment assistance: $100/month (up to $5,000 lifetime)

## 5. Time Off

*(See PTO and Leave Policy for full details)*
- Paid Time Off (PTO)
- Holidays (11 company-observed)
- Sick Leave
- Parental Leave
- Bereavement Leave
- Jury Duty Leave

## 6. Mental Health and Wellness

### 6.1 Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
- Free, confidential counseling: Up to 8 sessions per issue per year
- 24/7 crisis support line
- Resources for legal consultations, financial counseling, work-life balance

### 6.2 Mental Health Benefits
- Mental health parity: Behavioral health coverage equivalent to medical benefits
- Telehealth mental health visits available through medical plan

### 6.3 Wellness Reimbursement
- $500/year reimbursement for eligible wellness expenses:
  - Gym memberships, fitness equipment, wellness apps
  - Weight management programs
  - Smoking cessation programs

## 7. Additional Benefits

### 7.1 Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA)
- **Healthcare FSA:** Up to $3,200/year pre-tax for eligible medical expenses
- **Dependent Care FSA:** Up to $5,000/year pre-tax for qualifying dependent care expenses

### 7.2 Commuter Benefits
- Pre-tax parking: Up to $315/month
- Pre-tax transit: Up to $315/month

### 7.3 Professional Development
- Annual learning stipend: $1,500/year for courses, certifications, conferences
- Tuition reimbursement: Up to $5,250/year (undergraduate or graduate programs)

### 7.4 Employee Perks
- Corporate discount programs (retail, travel, entertainment)
- Free on-site amenities (varies by location)
- Employee referral bonus: $2,000 for successful referrals

## 8. Benefits Enrollment

### 8.1 New Hire Enrollment
- Must enroll within 30 days of hire date
- Enroll via the Benefits Portal at benefits.company.com
- Contact HR at benefits@company.com with questions

### 8.2 Annual Open Enrollment
- Held each November for benefits effective January 1
- Employees may change plans and coverage levels
- Reminder communications sent 3 weeks before open enrollment

### 8.3 Qualifying Life Events
Changes outside open enrollment are permitted within 30 days of:
- Marriage or divorce
- Birth or adoption of a child
- Loss of other coverage
- Change in dependent eligibility
